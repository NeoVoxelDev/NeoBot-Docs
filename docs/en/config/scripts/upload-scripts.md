# Upload Scripts
You can upload your NeoBot scripts here to extend the bot's functionality. Please ensure that the uploaded scripts comply with NeoBot's script specifications and have been fully tested to ensure their stability and security.

::: danger Warning
It is forbidden to upload scripts containing malicious code or infringing on others' copyrights, otherwise it may lead to civil or legal liability.
:::
::: info Information
This page lists community scripts. You can visit the [NeoBot Community Scripts Repository GitHub](https://github.com/NeoVoxelDev/NeoBotScriptsRepo) to submit issues or pull requests to apply for uploading your scripts!
:::
::: tip Tip
For uploading high-quality scripts, we will credit your scripts and recommend them in the community script repository!
:::
